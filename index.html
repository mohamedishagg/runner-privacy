1. Who We Are

Runner is a mobile application developed by Mohamed Salih, an independent developer based in the United Arab Emirates. Runner is a GPS-based territory capture game available on iOS. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using Runner, you agree to this policy.

Location Data

Location is the core function of Runner. Without it, the app cannot work. We collect your GPS coordinates while you are actively running or cycling to draw your path, detect zone captures, and show your live position to nearby players.
TypeWhen CollectedPurpose
Precise GPS (foreground)While the app is open and you are runningDraw run path, detect zone loops, show live position
Precise GPS (background)Only while an active run is in progressContinue tracking if you switch apps mid-run
Heat map pointsPeriodically during a runGenerate your personal heat map of covered areas

We do not collect location when the app is closed or when you are not actively running. Background location stops automatically when your run ends.

Device and Usage Data

  • Motion and fitness data (CoreMotion) — detects running vs cycling. Never stored on our servers.
  • Run statistics — distance, duration, territories captured — stored in your account
  • App interaction data — collected via Firebase Analytics to improve the app
  • Crash reports — collected via Firebase Crashlytics if the app crashes, to fix bugs

Payment Data

Runner Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see, store, or process your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs payment data.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion — delete your account in the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Correction — update your username, photo, or country in the app at any time
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format by emailing us
  • Withdraw consent — disable location or notifications at any time in iOS Settings

To exercise any right, email us at runner.main.admin@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Runner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us immediately and we will delete the account and all associated data.

8. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Firebase Authentication handles all password management — passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Firestore security rules restrict data so users can only access their own records
  • OTP email verification is required for all new accounts

9. International Data Transfers

Runner uses Google Firebase, which may store data on servers in the United States and other countries. Google Firebase complies with standard contractual clauses and appropriate transfer mechanisms. By using Runner, you consent to this transfer.

11. Push Notifications

Runner may send notifications for zone attacks, zone captures, friend requests, streak reminders, and heist alerts. You can disable all notifications in iOS Settings → Notifications → Runner. Attack alert notifications require Runner Pro.

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via a push notification or in-app message. Your continued use of Runner after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
