Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Runner ("we", "our", or "us") is a GPS-based territory capture game. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Location data — precise GPS coordinates while the app is in use, to draw your run path and capture territory on the map
  • Account information — email address and username you provide when creating an account
  • Usage data — run history, distance, pace, zones captured, and points earned
  • Profile photo — if you choose to upload one (optional)
  • Device data — device type and OS version for crash reporting

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your real-time position and run path on the map
  • To save captured territories and show them to other players
  • To calculate leaderboard rankings and crew scores
  • To send push notifications about zone attacks and game events
  • To verify your email address during account creation

Location Data

Runner uses your precise location only while the app is open and you are actively running. We do not track your location in the background without your knowledge. Your live location is visible to other users on the map while you are running, and is removed when you stop.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only in these circumstances:

  • Your username, zone colors, and captured territories are visible to all app users on the map
  • Your leaderboard ranking and points are publicly visible
  • We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and storage — subject to Google's privacy policy

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which permanently removes all your data including run history, territories, and account information.

Children's Privacy

Runner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw location permission at any time in iOS Settings

Security

We use Firebase's industry-standard security infrastructure. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are never stored — authentication is handled by Firebase Auth.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
