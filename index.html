Own-it Privacy Policy

We believe in complete transparency about how your data is handled.

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to Own-it, the GPS territory-capture game where you run and cycle real-world loops to claim zones, compete with rivals, and build your crew. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights over it. By using Own-it you agree to this policy.

Own-it is operated by the Own-it development team. If you have any questions at any point, reach us at support@runnerapp.run.

Own-it is designed primarily for users in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, and Egypt. We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area, and apply equivalent protections globally.

All data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure (Firebase). Here is how long we retain each category:

Territory & game state Until you delete your account

Location Data

Location is central to how Own-it works. Here is exactly how we use it:

During an active run or cycle
  • Continuous GPS tracking to draw your route
  • Territory zone capture detection
  • Your live position is shown on the shared map to other active players
  • Background location used to continue tracking if you switch apps
When app is open (not running)
  • Approximate location to centre the map
  • Show nearby territories and zones
  • Not sent to other users

GPS coordinates from completed runs are stored as part of your run history in Firebase Firestore. We do not sell or share location history with advertisers. You can delete individual runs or your entire account to remove all stored coordinates.

HealthKit & Apple Watch

If you connect an Apple Watch, Own-it requests access to HealthKit to read heart rate and activity data during workouts, and to save completed runs as workouts in the Health app.

What we read
  • Heart rate (live, during run)
  • Active calories
  • Activity ring data
What we write
  • Workout records (distance, time, calories)
  • Saved to your Apple Health app

HealthKit data is processed on your device and used only to display live stats during your run. We do not upload raw HealthKit data to our servers. Apple enforces strict HealthKit usage rules — we cannot use this data for advertising or share it with third parties.

Profile & Feed Photos

Uploading a profile photo or posting to the community feed is entirely optional.

Profile photo
  • Stored in Firebase Storage
  • Visible to all Own-it users on the map and leaderboard
  • Replaceable or removable any time from Profile settings
Feed posts
  • Photo and caption are optional per post
  • Stored in Firebase Storage and Firestore
  • Visible to all users in the community feed
  • You can delete individual posts at any time

Do not upload photos that include identifiable third parties without their consent. By uploading a photo you confirm you hold the rights to share it.

Push Notifications

Own-it sends push notifications for the following events:

Zone under attack Zone captured Streak reminders Weekly events Crew invitations Friend requests

You will be asked for notification permission during onboarding. You can change this at any time in your iPhone's Settings → Own-it → Notifications. Disabling notifications does not affect gameplay — you will still receive in-app alerts.

Subscriptions & Payments

Own-it Pro is available as a monthly or yearly subscription. All payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit 2. Own-it never sees, stores, or handles your payment card details.

We receive only a subscription status receipt from Apple (active / expired / trial) to unlock Pro features. Subscription management, refunds, and cancellations are handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Advertising

Free users of Own-it may encounter optional rewarded ads powered by Google AdMob. These ads are entirely opt-in — you actively choose to watch them in exchange for in-game rewards (such as bonus points or zone boosts). We never show interstitial or banner ads that interrupt gameplay.

Free Users
  • Optional rewarded ads only
  • You tap "Watch ad" to earn a reward
  • Never shown automatically
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers
Pro Subscribers
  • No ads of any kind
  • AdMob SDK inactive
  • No device identifiers collected for ads

AdMob may use device identifiers for ad personalization where permitted by your device settings. You can opt out at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iPhone. Opting out means you may still see ads but they will not be personalized to your interests. For AdMob's privacy policy visit policies.google.com/privacy.

Children

Own-it is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@runnerapp.run and we will delete the data promptly.

Users between 13 and 17 should obtain parental consent before creating an account. GPS-based features that share live location with other players require particularly careful parental consideration for minors.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and equivalent regional laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. All can be exercised by contacting us at support@runnerapp.run or through in-app settings where available:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data from the app
  • Export — download your run history and activity data
  • Correction — update your username, country, or language preference
  • Opt out of feed — choose not to post to or appear in the community feed
  • Notification opt-out — disable push notifications via iOS Settings
  • Restrict processing — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Lodge a complaint — complain to your local data protection authority (EU users: your national DPA)

We will respond to all data rights requests within 30 days. For account deletion, use Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the app, or email us and we will action it within 5 business days.

Cookies & Analytics

Own-it is a native iOS app and does not use browser cookies. Firebase may use persistent identifiers for analytics and crash reporting. These identifiers are not linked to your email or personal profile and are used solely to measure app stability and performance.

Free users may encounter optional rewarded ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers for ad personalization subject to your ATT (App Tracking Transparency) consent. If you decline tracking when prompted, or if you are a Pro subscriber, AdMob does not collect identifiers for personalization. We do not use any other ad networks or behavioural tracking.

Continued use of Own-it after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes. If you do not agree with a change, you may delete your account before the change takes effect.
